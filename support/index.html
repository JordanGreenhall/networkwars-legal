Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Commander Elite purchase?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Your purchase will be restored automatically using your App Store or Google Play account. Make sure you are signed in with the same account you used to make the original purchase.

How do I request a refund?

Refund requests must be made through the platform where you purchased:

  • Apple (iOS): reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Google (Android): Google Play Store support

We do not process payments directly and cannot issue refunds.

How do I delete my game data?

All local data is stored on your device. To delete it, uninstall the app. To request deletion of any server-side game logs associated with your Player ID, email us at privacy@starfighterstudios.com with your Player ID (found in the app's Settings screen).

Where can I find my Player ID?

Your Player ID appears in the app under Settings. It is a long alphanumeric identifier (UUID format). Include it in any support requests so we can look up your account.

Why isn't my leaderboard rank updating?

Leaderboard data is submitted at the end of each completed game. Check your internet connection, and ensure the game completed normally (not force-quit mid-game). If issues persist, email us with your Player ID and approximate time of gameplay.

Is NetworkWars available on both iOS and Android?

Yes. NetworkWars is available on the Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android).

Does NetworkWars have ads?

No. NetworkWars has no ads, no tracking SDKs, and no third-party analytics. The optional Commander Elite upgrade ($4.99, one-time) unlocks advanced features but is never required to play.
