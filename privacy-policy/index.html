NetworkWars Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026  |  Effective Date: April 1, 2026

Introduction

Starfighter Studios ("we," "us," or "our") operates the NetworkWars mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. NetworkWars is designed with a privacy-first approach: no ads, no third-party analytics, no tracking SDKs, and minimal data collection.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Generated Automatically

When you first launch NetworkWars, the App generates:

  • A local Player ID — a unique identifier (UUID) created on your device. This ID is not linked to your real identity, email address, or any other personal information. It is used solely to identify your game progress on your device.

1.2 Information You Provide

  • Nickname — If you choose to set a nickname (up to 16 characters), it is stored locally on your device and transmitted with game logs to our server. Nicknames are visible on leaderboards.

1.3 Gameplay Data

When you complete a game, the following data may be transmitted to our server:

  • Player ID and nickname
  • Game outcome (win, loss, surrender)
  • Game board state (node positions, army sizes)
  • Move-by-move game log (attack/defense actions)
  • Game duration and turn count
  • Game serial number (deterministic seed)
  • App version
  • Device type (phone or tablet)
  • Game speed and volume settings

This data is used to maintain leaderboards, detect bugs, improve game balance, and support tournament features.

1.4 Purchase Information

If you purchase the Commander Elite premium upgrade ($4.99 one-time), the following is stored locally on your device:

  • Purchase transaction ID (provided by Apple App Store or Google Play)
  • Purchase timestamp
  • Premium status flag

We do not process or store your payment information. All payment processing is handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We receive only a transaction confirmation.

1.5 Platform Account Linking (Optional)

You may optionally link your game to:

  • Apple Game Center (iOS) — We receive your Game Center player ID and display name.
  • Google Play Games (Android) — We receive your Google Play Games player ID and display name.

Platform linking is optional and used solely to enable platform-specific features (e.g., achievements). We do not transmit platform account IDs to our own servers.

1.6 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or mailing address
  • Your location (GPS, IP-based, or otherwise)
  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA, Google Advertising ID)
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Browsing history or data from other apps
  • Crash reports or diagnostics sent to third parties

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database within the App's sandboxed storage area. Sensitive data (such as purchase receipts) is stored in your device's secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).

3.2 Cloud Storage

Game log data is transmitted to our cloud server hosted on Microsoft Azure (United States). Data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption in transit) and authenticated with a secret key.

We retain game log data for as long as necessary to operate leaderboards, tournaments, and game balance analysis. We do not associate game log data with any personally identifiable information beyond your self-chosen nickname.

3.3 Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secret-key authentication for API endpoints
  • Sandboxed local storage on your device
  • Secure storage for sensitive data (purchase receipts)

5. Children's Privacy

NetworkWars is rated 4+ (iOS) / Everyone (Google Play). The App:

  • Does not collect personal information from children
  • Does not require account creation or email registration
  • Does not contain ads or links to external websites
  • Does not include social features, chat, or user-generated content that could expose children to other users
  • Does not use behavioral tracking or profiling

The only user-provided data is an optional nickname (up to 16 characters). Game data transmitted to our server contains no personally identifiable information beyond this self-chosen nickname.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

6.1 Local Data

All local data (player stats, badges, game history) is stored on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

6.2 Cloud Data

Game log data stored on our server is retained for as long as needed to operate game features (leaderboards, tournaments, balance analysis). To request deletion of your cloud data, contact us at the email below with your Player ID (found in the App's settings).

6.3 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Object to certain processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

7. International Data Transfers

Game log data is transmitted to and stored on servers in the United States (Microsoft Azure). By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your game data to the United States. We apply appropriate safeguards to protect your data regardless of where it is processed.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
